## OPPORTUNITY
- India has over 500 million smartphone users.
- Average internet speed is growing steadily (source: Geo Report 2020).
- About 65% of video content is consumed via mobile devices.

## RISK
- ARPU in India is approximately $2/month, compared to $12/month in the US.

## COMPETITOR INTELLIGENCE
- Local competitors dominate sports and regional content markets, e.g., Disney+ Hotstar.
